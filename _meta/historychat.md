# 📜 historychat.md — Complete Session History

> **Purpose:** The permanent master record of your entire learning journey.
> Two things live here: (1) a quick one-line table of every session ever, and (2) full summary blocks copied from `chatlog.md`.
> Use this to see your progress at a glance, and paste the last 2–3 entries into your AI at the start of any new session so it knows your history.

---

## 📋 How to Update This File

After **every** session:
1. Open `chatlog.md` and copy the completed `SUMMARY BLOCK`
2. Paste it at the top of the **SESSION HISTORY (Full Blocks)** section below (newest first)
3. Add one row to the **SESSION HISTORY TABLE**
4. Update the **Quick Stats** numbers
5. Update the **Streak Tracker**
6. Mark any **Milestones** you hit today
7. Run `git add . && git commit -m "session: [date] — [what I did]" && git push`

**To get a summary to paste here**, tell your AI at the end of a session:
> "Give me a 1-line summary of today's session for my historychat.md table. Format: session number, date, project, and what was accomplished in max 15 words."
